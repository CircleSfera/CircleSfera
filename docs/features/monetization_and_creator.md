# SaaS & Subscription Model

CircleSfera follows a 100% first-party SaaS model. Revenue is generated through tiered subscription plans that grant users access to premium features, enhanced analytics, and platform-wide benefits.

## 1. Subscription Tiers

The platform offers three distinct tiers designed for different levels of engagement:

### Premium (9.99€ / month)
The entry-level premium experience for users who want to elevate their profile.
- **Verification Badge**: Authentic blue checkmark on profile and search results.
- **Enhanced Profile**: Custom themes and bio formatting.
- **Basic Analytics**: Story views and post reach over time.
- **Ad-free Experience**: No internal promotions or algorithmic noise.
- **Priority Support**: Faster response times for help desk tickets.

### Elite Creator (19.99€ / month)
Designed for influential voices and digital artists.
- **Pro Growth Tools**: Advanced timing and audience insight tools.
- **Audience Breakdown**: Detailed demographics (region, active times).
- **Early Access**: Beta testing for new visual tools and filters.
- **Profile Spotlight**: Increased visibility in the "Discover" section.

### Business (49.99€ / month)
Optimized for brands, companies, and professional teams.
- **Business Verification**: Professional badge indicating a registered legal entity.
- **Management Tools**: Ability to manage multiple linked accounts.
- **Dedicated Account Manager**: Access to 24/7 direct support line.
- **API Access (Beta)**: Programmatic access to post metrics and feed data.

## 2. Platform Architecture

CircleSfera acts as the **Merchant of Record** for all transactions. 
- **Direct Billing**: All subscription payments are processed via Stripe Billing and go directly to the platform.
- **No Split Payments**: We do not facilitate peer-to-peer payments or revenue sharing with users.
- **No Third-Party Payouts**: There is no mechanism for user-to-user money transmission.

## 3. Compliance & Safety

- **Refund Policy**: Handled directly by CircleSfera support according to local consumer laws.
- **Security Check**: Active monitoring of accounts for subscription fraud.
- **Moderation**: Subscription does not exempt users from platform moderation policies; violations result in suspension without refund of the active period.
