Content Moderation Policy

CircleSfera

1.  Content Policy Overview

CircleSfera adopta un enfoque de Moderación Transparente y Basada en Ley. Eliminamos contenido que viola leyes españolas y de la UE, con decisiones justificadas por referencias legales específicas, no políticas corporativas arbitrarias.

Core Principles:

1.  Reglas claras y publicadas
2.  Enforcement consistente
3.  Decisiones transparentes con citas legales
4.  Proceso de apelaciones justo
5.  No shadowbanning

2\. Prohibited Content (Illegal)

2.1 Content That Will Be Removed

CSAM (Child Sexual Abuse Material)

- Severity: ZERO TOLERANCE
- Action: Instant removal + law enforcement report + account ban
- Detection: Hash matching + manual review
- No appeal possible

Terrorism & Violent Extremism

- Severity: ZERO TOLERANCE
- Action: Content removal + account ban
- Definition: Incitement to violence, terrorist recruitment
- Legal basis: Ley de Seguridad Nacional (Spain), DSA Article 16

Human Trafficking & Exploitation

- Severity: ZERO TOLERANCE
- Action: Removal + account ban + law enforcement report
- Includes: Sex trafficking, labor trafficking
- Legal basis: Article 177 Código Penal

Non-Consensual Intimate Images

- Severity: ZERO TOLERANCE
- Action: Instant removal (no appeal)
- Legal basis: Article 197 bis Código Penal (revenge porn)

Illegal Drug Trafficking

- Severity: ZERO TOLERANCE
- Action: Post removal + account ban
- Legal basis: Ley Orgánica 4/2015 de protección de la seguridad ciudadana

Counterfeit Goods

- Severity: High
- Action: Post removal
- Legal basis: Ley de Marcas

Hate Speech & Incitement to Violence

- Severity: High
- Action: Warning → removal → suspension
- Protected attributes: Race, ethnicity, religion, gender, sexual orientation, disability
- Legal basis: Article 510.1 Código Penal (delitos de odio)

Harassment & Bullying

- Severity: High
- Action: Warning → removal → suspension
- Definition: Repeated targeted attacks
- Legal basis: Article 173.1 Código Penal (acoso)

Misinformation (High-Impact False Claims)

- Severity: Medium
- Action: Labeling + potential removal (if harmful)
- Categories:

- Health: False treatments (NOT vaccine criticism)
- Elections: False voting procedures (NOT poll predictions)
- Safety: False emergency orders

Self-Harm & Suicide Content

- Severity: High
- Action: Content removal + crisis resources
- NOT removed: Prevention content, recovery stories

3\. Restricted Content (Legal but Limited)

3.1 Sensitive Content

Graphic Violence:

- Allowed if: Educational/journalistic, historical, art
- NOT allowed if: Glorification, instructions for violence

Alcohol & Tobacco:

- Not for sale/promotion
- Educational/news content OK

4\. Allowed Content (Protected Speech)

4.1 Political Speech

Fully Protected:

- Criticism of government
- Partisan campaigns
- Protest content
- Satire/parody

NOT allowed:

- Calls for violence
- False voting procedures
- Impersonation of officials

  4.2 Religious Content

Fully Protected:

- Criticism of religion
- Theological debate
- Religious expression

  4.3 LGBTQ+ Content

Fully Protected:

- Identity expression
- Activism
- Relationship content

5.1 Spam

5.2 Impersonation

Severity: High\

5.3 Copyright Infringement

Severity: Medium\

6\. Moderation Process

6.1 Automated Detection

Flow:

1.  Content uploaded

2\. Automated scanning:

- Text classification (OpenAI Moderation)

- Image analysis (custom model)

- Hash matching (known illegal)

- Keyword matching

3\. If flagged (>0.7 score):

- Mark for human review

- Content published (no queue)

4\. Moderator reviews < 24h

5\. Action taken + user notified

Automated Systems:

- OpenAI Moderation API (text)
- Custom TensorFlow model (images)
- Hash matching database

  6.2 Manual Moderation

Moderator Qualifications:

- Spanish fluency
- Legal training (4h)
- Policy knowledge (2h)
- Quarterly refresher

Review Process:

1.  Receive flagged content

2\. Review context/user history

3\. Decision:

- No action

- Warning

- Removal + legal citation

- Ban

4\. Notify user with appeal info

8\. Account Enforcement

Warning System:

- 1st: Warning + education
- 2nd: Removal + 24h suspension
- 3rd: 7-day suspension
- 4th: Permanent ban

Suspension:

- Cannot post/interact
- Profile visible
- Appeal available

9\. Appeal Process

Appeal Rights:

- All actions except CSAM
- Within 30 days
- Independent moderator
- Decision < 72h
- Transparent reasoning
